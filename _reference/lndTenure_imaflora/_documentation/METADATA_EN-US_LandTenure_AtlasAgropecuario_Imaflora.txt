id_imovel = Primary Key 
fonte = Source of rural property geometry
classe = Classify the rural property in private or public
		Terra pública = Public land
		Terra privada = Private land
		Urbano, água e transporte = Urban areas, water bodies ans transportation network
subclasse = Classify the rural property according to datasource
		Água = Water bodies 
		Área militar = Military areas
		Assentamento rural = Rural settlements
		CAR poor = CAR properties with more than 5% of overlapping areas with neighbours
		CAR premium = CAR properties with less than 5% of overlapping areas with neighbours
		Florestas tipo B = Undesignated public forests
 		SIGEF = Private properties registered in INCRA systems SIGEF/SNCI
		Terra indígena homologada = Homologated indigenous land
		Terra indígena não homologada = Non-homologated Indigenous land
		Terra legal não titulado = Undesignated lands from Terra Legal Program 
		Terra legal titulado = Private properties from Terra Legal Program 
		Terra Quilombola = Quilombola lands
		Território comunitário = Communitary lands
		Transporte = Transportation network 
		UC Proteção Integral  = Full Protection Conservation Unit
		UC Uso Sustentável = Sustainable Use Conservation Unit
		Urbano = Urban areas
area_orig = Polygon original area
area_final = Polygon fnial area after resolution overlapping areas
cd_mun = IBGE Municipalaity key / 5570 IDs
nm_mun = Municipality name / 5570 IDs
nm_micro = Microregion name/ 558 IDs
nm_meso = Mesoregion name/ 137 IDs
nm_regiao = Region names/ 5 IDs
nm_bioma = Name of the most representative biome where the rural property is inserted
tamanho = Rural properties size
		Pequeno = Small rural property (<= 4 fiscal modules)
		Médio = Medium rural property (area between 4 and 15 fiscal modules)
		Grande = Large rural property (> 15 fiscal modules)
		NA = Public and non-designated lands
nome = Filed with teh name of the polygon according to the datasource
